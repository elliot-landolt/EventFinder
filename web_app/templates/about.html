About Our App:

Our development team met as students at Georgetown University's McDonough School of Business. With interests in sporting events, live music, and travel, we were inspired to design an app that would allow users to know what events may be available to them in their chosen destinations. We hoped to connect our users with valuable and enjoyable experiences and ease the stress that can be involved with itinerary planning.
